Targeting

Reach the most relevant audience for your brand by focusing your ads in the right places. You can target Stack Exchange visitors by Geography, Reputation and Tags.
On the map to the left, the top 10 countries by visits are highlighted. Click on a country to see specific information about those visits.

Reputation is earned by users who ask and answer questions and their contribution is rated by peers.

A tag is a keyword or label that categorizes a question with other, similar questions. Questions can have multiple tags.

Advertising

Now that you’ve identified your target audience, let’s talk about how to get in front of them!

Banners

The Stack Exchange sites have 3 banner ad placements (SideBar, LeaderBoard and MiddleLeaderBoard) to feature your (static) creative.

Want to win in a specific category? Sponsor a Tag!

All questions on Stack Overflow are tagged to define the content of the question. Users can also receive tag updates via email – expanding your sponsorship exposure.

A tag sponsorship will give your brand logo placement on the tag icon (TM holders only) and all the questions featuring that tag. The tag homepage will showcase up to 5 text links and a 100x100 logo. The tag sponsor will also own a 50% share of all banner impressions targeted to the tag question and answer pages.
