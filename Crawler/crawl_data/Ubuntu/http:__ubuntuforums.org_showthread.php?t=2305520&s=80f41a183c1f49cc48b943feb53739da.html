    Re: Not Really New

    Ask your accountant for accounting/bookkeeping advice.
    If you like the advice, then ask computer gurus how to set it up so it's easy and safe.

    I value my relationship with my accountant. She keeps me out of trouble with the taxmen, social security, unemployment insurance, and a bunch of other authorities.
    She wants her customers to use QuickBooks...so I use QuickBooks in a VM.
    She knows how to guide her customers over the phone if they use QB.
    She knows how to pull the correct data from their company files in QB, quickly and efficiently.

    Sure I would like her to use something Ubuntu-compatible, but I'm not willing to pay her hourly rate for training and I'm not willing to change accountants right now. So I use what she wants, and it works.
    Your accountant, of course, may have different preferences.
